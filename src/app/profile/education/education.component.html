Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology

  • Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT) is an institute of repute in the state of Karnataka, founded by Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust in 1986.
  • The institute offers a wide range of B.E. degree programs including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Information Science, Bio-Technology, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and CSE (IoT & Cybersecurity including Blockchain Technology).
  • Sir MVIT also offers five Masters Programs, including M.Tech. Computer Integrated Manufacturing (CIM), M.Tech. in Electronics, M.Tech. in Biotechnology & Biochemical Engg, Masters Programs in Computer Applications, and Master of Business Administration.
